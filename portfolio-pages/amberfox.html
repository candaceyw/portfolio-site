Amber Fox Photographer
Branding and Website Design

Amber Fox is a talent photographer from Orange County, Californa. She was in need of a brand that fit her bright, spunky spirit. The scope of her project included a new logo and a website design.

The Project

Amber's last name was the perfect excuse to draw a cute fox as the stamp of her business. She really wanted the color scheme to be bright and cheerful - like a ray of sunshine, while keeping a modern, yet whimsical style.

Her website posed a certain challenge, she wanted a design and photography centric site to show off her incredible work but also easy to use when she needed to update content. We built her website using a WordPress theme from ProPhoto. She has a rather large body of work to show in her portfolio, but also needed space for her clients to get all the information about her services. She also needed a private client section to allow her clients to view private galleries and order additional prints. All this while keeping a responsive site that also looks great on mobile devices.
